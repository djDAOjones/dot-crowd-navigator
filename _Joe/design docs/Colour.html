Neutral palette

To accompany Nottingham Blue and the supporting palette, a small neutral palette can be used. This is made up of a light/warm grey and white.

These can be used on all designs where appropriate, and do not count towards the maximum two supporting colours per design and are permitted to make up over half of the designed asset, as these would be best used for text (white only) or backgrounds.

Portland Stone has a 40% tint available to create more accessible designs, more information on this can be found within the ‘digital backgrounds’ section.
